CARD BATTLE RULES:

Duel:

Losing card loses 10% of its power (rounded up) plus 1
Winning card gains 10% of the loser's power

Elimination

Losing card loses 50% of its power (rounded up) plus 1
Losing card removed from player's inventory 
Losing card added back to the packs
Winning card gains 50% of the loser's power

Capture

Losing card loses 1 power
Losing card removed from player's inventory
Losing card added to winning player's inventory
